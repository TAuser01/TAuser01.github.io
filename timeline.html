Key Eras

Victorian Era (1837-1901)

Many terraced houses were built during this time. Brick was a common material.

Edwardian Era (1901-1914)

Edwardian homes were larger with front gardens and big windows.

Post-war Housing (1945-1980)

More focus on practicality, with council estates becoming common.

Modern Era (1980-Present)

Homes today focus on energy efficiency and open spaces.
